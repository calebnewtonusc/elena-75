Who She Is

Her Story

The Beginning

Born on April 1, 1951, in the vibrant city of Manila, Philippines, Elena grew up shaped by the deep warmth of Filipino culture. She pursued her education at Saint Paul University Quezon City, forming lifelong bonds with her classmates that would span continents and decades.

Faith

Faith is the cornerstone of Elena's life. She is a devoted member of St. Rita Catholic Church in Tulare, CA and has long been part of prayer meetings, Wednesday night fellowships, and her church community. Her faith isn't a Sunday thing. It's how she lives every day.

A New Home

Elena made her life in Tulare, California, where she built a career at the County of Tulare. From Manila to the Central Valley, she carried her roots with her while building something entirely new.

Family First

Married to Bob Souza, Elena is the proud grandmother of Joshua, Ava, River, Olive, and more. Her grandchildren are the joy of her life. She shows up to every performance, every awards ceremony, every birthday. Every single time.
