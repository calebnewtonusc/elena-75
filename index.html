Who She Is

Her Story

Elena Ortencio Dejesus-Souza

Born April 1, 1951 in Manila, Philippines, Elena grew up shaped by the warmth of Filipino culture, a deep Catholic faith, and a love of people that never left her.

She pursued her degree at Saint Paul University Quezon City, where she formed bonds with classmates that would span continents and decades. In 2026, she was still having lunch with them in Manila.

Elena built her life in Tulare, California, where she worked at the County of Tulare, raised her family, and planted roots in her church community at St. Rita Catholic Church.

She married Bob Souza and raised two children: daughter Cathy and son Randy. Through them, she became a grandmother to six: Joshua, Ava, Caleb, and Selah (Cathy's), and River and Olive (Randy's). She shows up to every performance, every award, every milestone. Every single time.

At 75, she is still blowing out Tiramisu birthday cake at Wednesday night prayer meetings. Still going to Mass on Palm Sunday. Still treating her best friend to lunch. Still the center of every room she walks into.
